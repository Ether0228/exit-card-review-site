Q10 / KN6 / E04 / convert

A plane flies due west. What is its true bearing?

Rubric: Correct answer: 270°. (1 point for understanding bearing from north, 1 point for correct angle)

Sample strong answer

The true bearing of due west is 270°, because true bearing is measured clockwise from north, and west is 270° clockwise from north.

补救模块

E01

Thinks all quantities with numbers are scalars, ignoring direction.

Mini lesson: Scalars have only magnitude (size). Vectors have both magnitude and direction. Examples: mass (scalar), weight (vector).

E02

Thinks vectors must start at same point to be equal.

Mini lesson: Equal vectors have the same magnitude and direction, regardless of starting point. They are translations of each other.

E03

Thinks opposite vectors are just parallel or have different magnitudes.

Mini lesson: Opposite vectors have the same magnitude but exactly opposite directions (180° apart).

E04

Uses counterclockwise or starts from east.

Mini lesson: True bearing is measured clockwise from north, from 0° to 360°. North=0°, East=90°, South=180°, West=270°.

E05

Thinks AB and BA are the same vector.

Mini lesson: Vector AB starts at A and ends at B. Vector BA starts at B and ends at A. They have the same magnitude but opposite direction.
