Sample strong answer

The function increases on (-∞,1), decreases on (1,3), then increases on (3,∞), so it has a local maximum at (1,4) and a local minimum at (3,0). It is concave down on (-∞,2), concave up on (2,∞), and has an inflection point at (2,2).

补救模块

E1

Does not know how to find domain of polynomial functions.

Mini lesson: The domain of a function is all x-values for which it is defined. Polynomials are defined for all real numbers because you can plug in any x and get a real output.

E2

Cannot find x-intercepts by solving f(x)=0.

Mini lesson: To find x-intercepts, set y=0 and solve for x. Factor if possible.

E3

Makes errors in power rule differentiation.

Mini lesson: Use power rule: d/dx (x^n) = n x^(n-1). Differentiate term by term.

E4

Cannot solve f'(x)=0 to find critical points.

Mini lesson: Critical points occur where f'(x)=0 or f'(x) undefined. Solve f'(x)=0 by factoring or quadratic formula.

E5

Misinterprets sign of f' for increasing/decreasing.

Mini lesson: If f'(x)>0 on an interval, f is increasing. If f'(x)<0, f is decreasing. Test points in each interval between critical points.

E6

Cannot compute second derivative correctly.

Mini lesson: Second derivative is the derivative of the first derivative. Apply power rule again.

E7

Cannot find inflection points from f''(x)=0.

Mini lesson: Inflection points occur where f''(x)=0 or undefined and concavity changes. Solve f''(x)=0.

E8

Misinterprets sign of f'' for concavity.

Mini lesson: If f''(x)>0, f is concave up (cup-shaped). If f''(x)<0, f is concave down (cap-shaped).

E9

Cannot relate graphs of f, f', f''.

Mini lesson: Where f has a local max/min, f' crosses x-axis. Where f' has local max/min, f'' crosses x-axis. f' positive means f increasing.

E10

Cannot synthesize analysis into a sketch.

Mini lesson: Plot intercepts, critical points, inflection points. Draw curve following increasing/decreasing and concavity intervals.
